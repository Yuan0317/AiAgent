# **Golden Egg Foo Young**
  
### Ingredients:
- 4 large eggs
- 1/2 cup of shredded cabbage
- 1/4 cup of chopped spring onions
- 1/4 cup of sliced mushrooms
- 1/4 cup of bean sprouts
- 1/4 cup of diced cooked chicken or shrimp
- 2 tablespoons of soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
- 1 teaspoon of sesame oil
- 1/2 teaspoon of sugar
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Vegetable oil for frying

### Instructions:
1. **Prepare the Egg Mixture:**
   - In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs and season with soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar, salt, and pepper.
   
2. **Add the Fillings:**
   - Mix in the shredded cabbage, chopped spring onions, sliced mushrooms, bean sprouts, and diced chicken or shrimp.
   
3. **Cook the Egg Foo Young:**
   - Heat some vegetable oil in a non-stick frying pan over medium heat.
   - Pour a ladleful of the egg mixture into the pan to form a small omelette.
   - Cook for 2-3 minutes until the bottom is set and golden brown.
   - Carefully flip the egg foo young and cook the other side for an additional 2-3 minutes.
   
4. **Serve:**
   - Remove the golden egg foo young from the pan and place on a serving plate.
   - Repeat the process with the remaining egg mixture to make more egg foo young.
   
5. **Garnish and Enjoy:**
   - Garnish the egg foo young with extra spring onions and a drizzle of soy sauce.
   - Serve hot with steamed rice and enjoy your homemade golden egg foo young!

### Cooking Tips:
- Make sure the frying pan is well-heated before pouring the egg mixture to achieve a nice golden color.
- Customize the fillings based on your preferences, such as adding shrimp, pork, or different vegetables.
- You can also make a simple gravy by combining chicken broth, soy sauce, and cornstarch to drizzle over the egg foo young for extra flavor.

### Estimated Time:
- Preparation: 15 minutes
- Cooking: 15 minutes
- Total Time: 30 minutes
