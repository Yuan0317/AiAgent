# **Spicy Pepper Egg Drop Stir Fry**

### **Ingredients:**
- 3 eggs
- 2 red peppers, sliced
- 2 green peppers, sliced
- 1 tsp vegetable oil
- 1 tsp soy sauce
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 1/4 tsp salt
- 1/4 tsp pepper
- 1/2 tsp cornstarch
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 inch ginger, minced
- 1 green onion, chopped

### **Instructions:**
1. **Prepare the Ingredients:**
   - Beat the eggs in a bowl and set aside.
   - In a small bowl, mix soy sauce, sugar, salt, pepper, and cornstarch. Set aside.
   - Slice the red and green peppers, mince the garlic and ginger, and chop the green onion.

2. **Cooking Process:**
   1. Heat the vegetable oil in a pan over medium heat.
   2. Add the minced garlic and ginger, and sauté until fragrant.
   3. Add the sliced peppers and stir fry for about 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
   4. Push the peppers to the side of the pan and pour in the beaten eggs. Let them set slightly before gently scrambling them.
   5. Once the eggs are cooked, mix them with the peppers.
   6. Pour the soy sauce mixture over the eggs and peppers, stirring well to combine.
   7. Add the chopped green onion and stir fry for another minute.
   8. Adjust seasoning if needed.

3. **Serve:**
   - Transfer the Spicy Pepper Egg Drop Stir Fry to a serving plate.
   - Garnish with additional chopped green onions if desired.
   - Serve hot with steamed rice.

### **Cooking Tips and Tricks:**
- **Control the Heat:** Ensure the pan is not too hot when adding the eggs to prevent them from overcooking.
- **Keep Stirring:** Constant stirring will help create a nice texture for the egg drop.
- **Personalize:** Feel free to add other vegetables or protein like shrimp or chicken for more variation.

### **Estimated Time:**
- **Preparation Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cooking Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 20 minutes
