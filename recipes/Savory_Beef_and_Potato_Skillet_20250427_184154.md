# **Savory Beef and Potato Skillet**

## Ingredients:
- 1 lb beef sirloin, thinly sliced
- 4 medium potatoes, diced
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 1/2 cup beef broth
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 teaspoon paprika
- 1 teaspoon dried thyme
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Fresh parsley for garnish

## Instructions:

### 1. Preparing the Ingredients:
1. Season the beef slices with salt, pepper, paprika, and thyme. Set aside.
2. Heat olive oil in a large skillet over medium-high heat.

### 2. Cooking the Potatoes:
1. Add the diced potatoes to the skillet and cook for about 8-10 minutes, stirring occasionally, until they start to brown and become tender.
2. Remove the potatoes from the skillet and set aside.

### 3. Cooking the Beef:
1. In the same skillet, add the seasoned beef slices and cook for 3-4 minutes on each side until browned.
2. Remove the beef from the skillet and set aside.

### 4. Sauteing the Aromatics:
1. In the same skillet, add the diced onion and minced garlic. Cook for 2-3 minutes until the onions are translucent and fragrant.

### 5. Combining Ingredients:
1. Return the cooked potatoes and beef to the skillet.
2. Pour in the beef broth and stir to combine all ingredients.
3. Allow the mixture to simmer for another 5-7 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.

### 6. Serving:
1. Garnish with fresh parsley before serving.
2. Enjoy your savory beef and potato skillet hot.

## Cooking Tips and Tricks:
- Make sure to slice the beef thinly to ensure quick and even cooking.
- You can customize the seasoning with your favorite herbs and spices for added flavor.
- Feel free to add vegetables like bell peppers or carrots for extra color and nutrients.

## Estimated Time:
- Preparation: 15 minutes
- Cooking: 25 minutes
- Total: 40 minutes
