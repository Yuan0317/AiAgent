# **Golden Egg Foo Young**

## Ingredients:
- 4 eggs
- 1/2 cup diced cooked ham
- 1/4 cup chopped green onions
- 1/4 cup bean sprouts
- 1/4 cup shredded carrots
- 1/4 cup sliced mushrooms
- 1/4 cup water chestnuts, sliced
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- 1 teaspoon sesame oil
- 1/2 teaspoon sugar
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- Vegetable oil for frying
- Chopped green onions for garnish

## Instructions:

1. **Preparation:**
   - In a bowl, beat the eggs and set aside.
   - Prepare all the vegetables and ham as listed in the ingredients.
   - Mix the soy sauce, oyster sauce, sesame oil, sugar, and white pepper in a small bowl to make the sauce.

2. **Cooking:**
   1. Heat some vegetable oil in a non-stick skillet over medium heat.
   2. Add the ham, green onions, bean sprouts, carrots, mushrooms, and water chestnuts. Stir-fry for 2-3 minutes until vegetables are slightly softened.
   3. Push the vegetables to one side of the skillet and pour the beaten eggs into the empty side.
   4. Let the eggs cook for a minute until the edges start to set.
   5. Gently fold the vegetables into the eggs to form a loose omelet.
   6. Cook for another minute until the eggs are fully set.

3. **Assembly:**
   1. Pour the prepared sauce over the egg foo young.
   2. Garnish with chopped green onions.
   3. Serve hot with steamed rice or noodles.

## Cooking Tips:
- Make sure the skillet is hot before adding the ingredients to achieve a nice sear.
- Do not overcook the vegetables, they should be slightly crunchy for texture.
- You can add other protein options like shrimp, chicken, or tofu based on your preference.

## Estimated Time:
- Preparation: 15 minutes
- Cooking: 10 minutes
- Total Time: 25 minutes
