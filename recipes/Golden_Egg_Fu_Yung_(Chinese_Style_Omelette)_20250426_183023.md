# **Golden Egg Fu Yung (Chinese Style Omelette)**

### Ingredients:
- 4 eggs
- 1 small onion, finely chopped
- 200g chicken breast, thinly sliced
- 2 tbsp soy sauce
- 1 tsp sesame oil
- 1/2 tsp white pepper
- 1/2 tsp sugar
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- Salt, to taste
- Chopped spring onions for garnish

### Instructions:

1. **Prepare the Ingredients:**
   - Beat the eggs in a bowl and set aside.
   - Marinate the chicken slices with soy sauce, sesame oil, white pepper, and sugar for 15 minutes.
   - Chop the onion finely.

2. **Cooking the Omelette:**
   1. Heat 1 tablespoon of vegetable oil in a non-stick pan over medium heat.
   2. Add the marinated chicken slices and stir-fry until cooked through. Remove from the pan and set aside.
   3. In the same pan, add the remaining tablespoon of oil and sauté the chopped onions until translucent.
   4. Pour the beaten eggs into the pan over the onions.
   5. Add the cooked chicken back into the pan and gently fold the omelette over the filling. Cook until the eggs are set.

3. **Finishing Touches:**
   - Season the omelette with salt to taste.
   - Slide the omelette onto a serving plate.
   - Garnish with chopped spring onions.

4. **Cooking Tips and Tricks:**
   - Be sure not to overcook the eggs to keep them moist and tender.
   - You can add other vegetables like peas, carrots, or bell peppers for added color and flavor.
   - For a spicier kick, you can add a dash of hot sauce or chili flakes.

5. **Estimated Time:**
   - Preparation Time: 15 minutes
   - Cooking Time: 15 minutes
   - Total Time: 30 minutes

Enjoy your delicious Golden Egg Fu Yung, a classic Chinese dish that's perfect for a quick and satisfying meal!
