# **Chinese Tomato and Egg Stir-Fry**

## **Ingredients:**
- 4 large eggs
- 2 medium-sized tomatoes
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 1 tablespoon soy sauce
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1/2 teaspoon salt
- 1/4 teaspoon white pepper
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 2 green onions, chopped for garnish

## **Instructions:**
1. **Prepare the Ingredients:**
   - Beat the eggs in a bowl and set aside.
   - Cut the tomatoes into wedges and set aside.
   - Mix the soy sauce, sugar, salt, and white pepper in a small bowl and set aside.

2. **Cooking Process:**
   1. Heat the vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over medium heat.
   2. Add the minced garlic and stir-fry for about 30 seconds until fragrant.
   3. Add the tomato wedges and stir-fry for about 2-3 minutes until they start to soften.
   4. Push the tomatoes to the side of the wok, pour in the beaten eggs, and let them set for a few seconds.
   5. Gently scramble the eggs until they are about 80% cooked, then mix them with the tomatoes.
   6. Pour the soy sauce mixture over the tomato and egg mixture and stir well to combine.
   7. Continue cooking for another 1-2 minutes until the eggs are fully cooked and the sauce has thickened slightly.
   
3. **Serve:**
   - Transfer the tomato and egg stir-fry to a serving plate.
   - Garnish with chopped green onions.
   - Serve hot with steamed rice.

## **Cooking Tips and Tricks:**
- **Tomato Selection:** Choose ripe but firm tomatoes for better texture in the dish.
- **Egg Texture:** For a silkier texture, you can beat the eggs with a splash of water or chicken broth.
- **Sauce Adjustment:** Adjust the seasoning according to your taste preference by adding more soy sauce or sugar if needed.
- **Quick Cooking:** Be sure not to overcook the eggs to maintain their tenderness.

## **Estimated Time:**
- **Preparation Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cooking Time:** 10 minutes
- **Total Time:** 20 minutes
