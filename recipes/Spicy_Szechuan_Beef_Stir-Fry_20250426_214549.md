# **Spicy Szechuan Beef Stir-Fry**

### **Ingredients:**
- 500g beef, thinly sliced
- 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
- 3 cloves garlic, minced
- 1-inch piece of ginger, minced
- 1 small red bell pepper, sliced
- 1 small green bell pepper, sliced
- 2-3 dried red chilies, chopped
- 2 tablespoons soy sauce
- 1 tablespoon oyster sauce
- 1 tablespoon hoisin sauce
- 1 teaspoon sugar
- 1 teaspoon cornstarch
- 1/2 cup beef broth
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Spring onions, chopped for garnish

### **Instructions:**
1. **Marinate the Beef:**
   - In a bowl, mix the beef slices with soy sauce, sugar, and cornstarch. Let it marinate for at least 30 minutes.

2. **Prepare the Sauce:**
   - In a separate bowl, mix soy sauce, oyster sauce, hoisin sauce, and beef broth. Set aside.

3. **Stir-Fry:**
   - Heat vegetable oil in a wok or large skillet over high heat.
   - Add minced garlic, ginger, and dried red chilies. Stir-fry for a minute until fragrant.
   - Add the marinated beef slices and stir-fry until browned on all sides.
   - Add the sliced bell peppers and continue to stir-fry for another 2-3 minutes.

4. **Add the Sauce:**
   - Pour the prepared sauce over the beef and vegetables.
   - Season with salt and pepper, and stir well to combine.
   - Cook for another 2-3 minutes until the sauce thickens and coats the beef evenly.

5. **Finish and Serve:**
   - Garnish with chopped spring onions.
   - Serve hot over steamed rice or noodles.

### **Cooking Tips:**
- Ensure the beef slices are thinly cut to ensure quick and even cooking.
- Adjust the amount of dried red chilies based on your spice preference.
- Stir-fry over high heat to achieve that characteristic smoky flavor in Chinese dishes.

### **Estimated Time:**
- Preparation Time: 15 minutes
- Cooking Time: 15 minutes
- Total Time: 30 minutes
