# **Silky Strawberry Egg Pudding**

## **Ingredients:**
- 4 eggs
- 1 cup milk
- 1/2 cup strawberries, finely chopped
- 3 tbsp sugar
- 1 tsp vanilla extract

## **Preparation:**
1. **Prepare the Custard Mixture:**
   - In a mixing bowl, beat the eggs lightly.
   - Add milk, sugar, and vanilla extract. Mix well until sugar is dissolved.
   - Strain the mixture to ensure a smooth consistency.

2. **Prepare the Strawberry Puree:**
   - Blend the chopped strawberries until smooth.
   - Strain the puree to remove seeds.

## **Cooking Instructions:**
1. **Prepare the Steamer:**
   - Fill a steamer pot with water and bring it to a boil.

2. **Assemble the Pudding:**
   - Divide the strawberry puree equally among the serving cups.
   - Slowly pour the custard mixture over the strawberry puree.

3. **Steam the Puddings:**
   - Place the filled cups in the steamer.
   - Steam over medium heat for about 15-20 minutes or until the pudding is set.

4. **Chill and Serve:**
   - Remove the pudding cups from the steamer and let them cool.
   - Refrigerate for at least 2 hours before serving.

## **Cooking Tips and Tricks:**
- **Straining:** Straining the custard mixture and strawberry puree helps in achieving a smooth texture for the pudding.
- **Steamer:** Make sure the water in the steamer is gently boiling to ensure even cooking.
- **Chilling:** Chilling the pudding enhances the flavors and sets the texture perfectly.

## **Estimated Time:**
- **Preparation Time:** 15 minutes
- **Cooking Time:** 20 minutes
- **Chilling Time:** 2 hours
- **Total Time:** Approximately 2 hours 35 minutes
